1. Overview

ViDiary is a personal journaling application that prompts users to create short video entries during the day. Depending on the version and features you enable, the app may store your entries locally on your device and may process certain technical or account-related information necessary to operate the service.

Your videos and diary content are intended for your personal use. The app is not designed as a public social network.

2. Data We Collect

Depending on the features you use, ViDiary may collect or process the following categories of data:

  • Account information - such as your email address, sign-in provider identifier, or profile name, if you sign in using Google, Apple, or another authentication provider.
  • User-created content - including short video diary entries, optional captions, tags, timestamps, and related metadata.
  • Device permissions and local files - such as camera, microphone, notifications, and access to media storage where required for the app to function.
  • Technical and diagnostic data - such as app version, device model, operating system, crash logs, and performance information.
  • Subscription or purchase status - if you offer premium features through app stores or third-party billing services.

Note: If videos are stored only on the user’s device and are not uploaded to external servers, make that explicit here and in the store listing.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide and operate ViDiary features.
  • To let you record, save, organize, and review your diary entries.
  • To send local reminders or notifications you enable.
  • To maintain account access and subscription status, where applicable.
  • To troubleshoot bugs, improve stability, and protect the app against misuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data only in the following situations:

  • Service providers - for authentication, analytics, crash reporting, subscription management, hosting, or other infrastructure support.
  • App store or payment platforms - such as Google Play or Apple App Store, when needed to process purchases and subscriptions.
  • Legal compliance - if disclosure is required by law, legal process, or a valid government request.
  • Business transfer - if the app or developer business is merged, acquired, or sold.

If ViDiary stores entries exclusively on-device and does not upload videos to remote servers, then user video content is not shared externally except where the user explicitly exports or shares it.

5. Data Storage, Retention, and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no method of storage, transmission, or security is perfect. In other words, absolute security is one of humanity’s favorite fairy tales.

  • Local storage - diary videos may be stored on your device.
  • Cloud or backend storage - if enabled in your product architecture, certain account or technical data may be processed on secure external infrastructure.
  • Retention - we retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

You are responsible for protecting access to your device and any accounts used to access the app.

6. Permissions

ViDiary may request the following permissions, depending on platform and enabled features:

  • Camera - to record diary videos.
  • Microphone - to capture audio with your recordings.
  • Notifications - to remind you to record moments during the day.
  • Photos / Media / Files - to save, read, export, or compile video content where required.

You can usually manage app permissions through your device settings. Disabling some permissions may limit app functionality.

7. International Data Transfers

If any personal data is processed outside your country of residence, we will take reasonable steps to ensure such data is protected in accordance with applicable law.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Request data portability where applicable.

If your data is stored locally on your device only, many deletion and control actions may be performed directly by uninstalling the app or deleting content within the app.

9. Children’s Privacy

ViDiary is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law in their jurisdiction unless specifically stated otherwise. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law.
