Privacy Policy

1. Overview

PrivoDiary is a personal journaling application that prompts users to create short video entries during the day. The app is designed primarily to operate locally on the user’s device.

Video diary entries and related user content are stored locally on the user’s device unless the user explicitly chooses to export or share them. PrivoDiary does not upload user video content to external servers as part of its core functionality.

Certain limited account-related data may be processed in connection with Google Sign-In and subscription or app access features, where applicable.

2. Data We Collect

PrivoDiary may process limited categories of data depending on the features you use:

  • Account information - such as your email address, Google account identifier, display name, and basic profile information provided through Google Sign-In.
  • User-created content - short video diary entries, optional captions, timestamps, and related metadata stored locally on your device.
  • Device permissions - such as camera, microphone, notifications, and media access, used to enable the app’s functionality on your device.
  • Subscription or access status - information necessary to determine whether premium features are available to your account.

PrivoDiary does not upload or store user video diary content on external servers as part of its core functionality.

3. How We Use Data

  • To authenticate users through Google Sign-In.
  • To provide access to app features, including premium functionality where applicable.
  • To let you record, save, organize, and review diary entries on your device.
  • To send local reminders or notifications that you enable.
  • To compile recorded clips into a daily video on your device.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

PrivoDiary does not sell user personal data.

User-created video diary content remains stored locally on the device and is not shared by PrivoDiary with third parties as part of the app’s core functionality.

Certain account-related data may be processed by third-party providers where necessary to support sign-in, app distribution, subscription management, or payment processing, including Google and app store platforms, in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

If you choose to export, transfer, back up, or share your content using third-party apps, cloud services, social networks, file managers, or device tools, such actions are performed at your own discretion and responsibility.

PrivoDiary is not responsible for disclosure, loss, or unauthorized access resulting from user-initiated sharing, insecure device handling, third-party services chosen by the user, or compromise of the user’s device or accounts.

5. Data Storage, Retention, and Security

Video diary content is stored locally on your device. PrivoDiary does not maintain external storage of your video diary content as part of its core functionality.

Limited account-related information associated with Google Sign-In or subscription status may be processed as necessary to provide access to the app and its features.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. However, no method of storage, transmission, or security system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Therefore, absolute security cannot be assured.

  • Local storage - diary videos and related content are stored on your device.
  • Retention - locally stored content remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, or otherwise remove it.

You are responsible for protecting access to your device, your Google account, exported files, backups, and any third-party services you use.

6. Permissions

PrivoDiary may request the following permissions, depending on platform and enabled features:

  • Camera - to record diary videos.
  • Microphone - to capture audio with your recordings.
  • Notifications - to remind you to record moments during the day.
  • Photos / Media / Files - to save, read, export, or compile video content where required.

You can usually manage app permissions through your device settings. Disabling some permissions may limit app functionality.

7. International Data Transfers

PrivoDiary does not transfer personal data internationally because the app does not collect or transmit personal data to external servers.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access personal data processed in connection with your account.
  • Request correction of inaccurate account data.
  • Request deletion of personal data where applicable.
  • Delete locally stored diary content directly from the app or your device.
  • Stop using the app at any time.

9. Children’s Privacy

PrivoDiary is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law in their jurisdiction unless specifically stated otherwise. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any other users.

10. Third-Party Services

PrivoDiary may rely on third-party services where necessary to support app functionality, including Google Sign-In, Google Play, and Google Play Billing.

Such third-party services may process personal data in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
